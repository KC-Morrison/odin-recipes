Cinnamon Toast

Description

Great for breakfast, dessert, or snack time!

Ingredients

  • 1 slice whole wheat bread
  • 1 tablespoon buttery spread
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 rose petal (Optional)

Steps

  1. Toast slice of bread to desired crispness, 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Spread buttery spread across the slice.
  3. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar evenly over toast
  4. Decorate with a rose petal.
